BBC R&D are collaborating with Global Invacom Ltd to research the use of a broadcast Wi-Fi system.

Broadcast Wi-Fi allows content to be received and consumed on mobiles, without the need to connect to mobile networks.

The BBC is particularly interested in exploring how broadcast
Wi-Fi can be used to view audio visual content at events.

To take part in this trial you will need to install Global Invacom's app which is available to download from both android and iOS app store(s) and can be reached by clicking the relevant link below.



By downloading and using Global Invacom's app you will be agreeing to Global Invacom's terms of use and its privacy policy. By taking part in the trial you agree to provide anonymised performance data, which the BBC intends to use for assessing the performance of the broadcast Wi-Fi system. The BBC may share this data with its trusted third parties, including Global Invacom. By taking part in this trial you will also be agreeing to the BBC's On-Line Terms of Use.
